Pokemon Battle Arena

A game based on the classic pokemon game, this game is a turn based game between two players who choose from a selection of pokemon to see who is the last player standing. Technologies: HTML5, CSS3, Javascript / jQuery

Pocket Trackr

A web application where users can input expenses and income and get visual representations of how they are spending their money.

Technologies: Ruby on Rails, HTML, CSS, Javascript / jQuery, Git/Github and Heroku.

Partmint

A web application where property owners can list their properties online and chat with their tenants in real-time. Residents can also chat with their property owners as well as pay for their monthly rent through a secure payment gateway which utilizes the Stripe API. General users can also browse through a gallery of vacant properties and contact property owners through a form for general inquiries. My role in the team was developer and Trello board task coordinator.

Technologies: HTML5, CSS3, Javascript, jQuery, Node.js, Express, Passport.js, MongoDB, Git/Github, Heroku

Pressto

A food and beverage ordering web application where users can purchase from a selection of menu items and pay using a debit card/credit card through a secure payment gateway. After, users can keep track of the status of their order in real time to know when their order is ready for pickup. As logged in administrators, they can keep track of the order history, manage the products on the menu and update the status of the orders in real time.

Technologies: HTML5, CSS3, Javascript, React.js, Node.js, Express, Stripe API, Socket.io, MongoDB, JSON Web Tokens
